Located near Batha Museum and Bab Boujloud, RIAD FES AUTHENTIC PALACE - SPA offers you an unforgettable stay.

The Riad has been restored in the pure Moroccan tradition which testifies to the richness and the refinement of the Arabo-Andalusian architecture : Zellige, carved plaster and hand carved cedar wood marry in harmony, true illustration of diversity, the richness of traditions and Moroccan culture deeply rooted in the history of this fascinating and enchanting country ...
