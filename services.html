Our Services

SPA

Wellbeing at RIAD FES AUTHENTIC PALACE - SPA. Relax in our newly created hammam and Spa, bliss out with a massage and indulge yourself with our treatments using local organic products, essential oils and argan oil. Guests not staying at the riad are also welcome, it’s perfect for couples or friends. Toiletries and towels are available for free.

Hammam

Take some time to relax and rejuvenate in our authentic Moroccan Hammam, elegantly decorated with zellige (mosaic tiles) and tadelakt. The heat from the floor, walls and tiled benches will open your pores, cleanse your skin and offer a deep sense of relaxation
– 28 € per person
– 45 minutes

Gommage

After heating up, your skin is ready for cleansing. Gommage is the traditional exfoliation treatment using a black eucalyptus and olive oil soap. The whole body is scrubbed with an exfoliating glove for deep cleansing and skin stimulation. This is followed by a hairwash and natural soap rub after many rinsing
– 38 € per person
– 60 minutes

Rassoul

For the ultimate treatment, have your body coated in clay after the gommage to extract toxins and soften the skin. Add a Hair Treatment with Argan oil, shampoo and conditioner for an extra 50 dirhams. You can buy a bottle of Argan oil after the Hammam to moisturise your skin and take home.
– 42 € per person
– 60 minutes

Massage

Float away with a blissful relaxation massage with either Moroccan Argan oil or orange flower oil. It doesn’t get better than this!
– 40 € per person
– 45 minutes
