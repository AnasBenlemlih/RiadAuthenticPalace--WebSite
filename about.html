Présentation

Located near Batha Museum and Bab Boujloud,

RIAD FES AUTHENTIC PALACE - SPA offers you an unforgettable stay.

The Riad has been restored in the pure Moroccan tradition which testifies to the richness and the refinement of the Arabo-Andalusian architecture : Zellige, carved plaster and hand carved cedar wood marry in harmony, true illustration of diversity, the richness of traditions and Moroccan culture deeply rooted in the history of this fascinating and enchanting country, common spaces loaded with privacy and discretion with subdued lighting that reproduce the fascinating and overflowing atmosphere of oriental magic. This charming setting shelters around a patio decorated with a fountain: 6 rooms and 7 suites, a Moroccan restaurant and a SPA (hammam, massage). It is a real immersion in Moroccan culture that is offered to you.
